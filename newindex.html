Welcome to Hasegawa Pottery

Welcome to a place where tradition and innovation meet. At Hasegawa Pottery, we offer tea utensils and everyday pottery, each piece carefully crafted by hand, carrying centuries of Japanese artisanry into your home.

About Our Heritage

Our store was founded in the Showa period, nestled in the historic city of Kyoto. Today, the current owner proudly serves as the nth-generation head, continuing a legacy of excellence. We specialize in exquisite tea bowls and a wide range of traditional tea utensils. Hasegawa Pottery meticulously honors the profound aesthetics of Japanese culture and the serene spirit of the tea ceremony, while simultaneously creating pieces that fit harmoniously into modern life and contemporary spaces.

Our Exquisite Products

  • Raku Tea Bowl: Hand-formed, unique pieces embodying wabi-sabi aesthetics, ideal for the tranquil tea ceremony.
  • Chasen Holder: Elegantly designed stands to preserve the shape and extend the life of your bamboo tea whisk.
  • Fresh Water Container (Mizusashi): Beautifully crafted vessels essential for the tea ceremony, holding pure water for replenishing the kettle.
  • Matcha Whisk (Chasen): Meticulously carved bamboo whisks, perfect for preparing frothy matcha.
  • Tea Scoop (Chashaku): Delicately carved scoops for precise measurement of matcha powder.
