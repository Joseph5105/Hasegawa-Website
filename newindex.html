Welcome to Hasegawa Pottery

Welcome to a place where tradition and innovation meet. At Hasegawa Pottery, we offer tea utensils and everyday pottery, each piece carefully crafted by hand.

About

Our store was founded in the Showa period, Kyoto. Today, "Owner" serves as the nth-generation head. We specialize in tea bowls and a wide range of tea utensils. Hasegawa Pottery honors the aesthetics of Japanese culture and the spirit of tea ceremony while creating pieces that fit harmoniously into modern life.
